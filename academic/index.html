Huaneng Chen
M.S. student @ Xiamen University

I am a first-year Master of Science (M.S.) student in Management Science at Xiamen University, currently conducting research under the supervision of Shan Jiang. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Management Science, also from Xiamen University.

My research interests span operations research and machine learning, with a current focus on the flying sidekick traveling salesman problem with multiple drops (FSTSP-MD, a variant of the classic TSP).

Outside of my academic work, I am a passionate self-learner with a deep interest in diverse areas of computer science and mathematics. I maintain a GitHub repository called Notes and a blog here to share my self-learning notes and experiences.
